Year 1 vs Year 3 – Creature Design Glow Up

Comparing my first enemy design from 3 years ago with my current version, plus what I changed in shape language, anatomy, and color.

Creature Design Before / After Game Art
Read full post

When I first designed this enemy, I focused mostly on cool details, not on a clear silhouette. The result was messy and hard to read, especially at small sizes in-game. Three years later, I redesigned it with stronger shapes and a clearer elemental theme.

Before (Year 1)
Weak silhouette, noisy details, and unclear focal point.
After (Year 3)
Clearer silhouette, simplified shapes, and focused color palette for the elemental theme.

I also changed the pose to better show the creature’s weight and aggression. This redesign taught me to thumbnail silhouettes first before adding details.
